Product overview

ARBoard — AI Architecture Review Board

A multi-agent AI platform that runs expert Salesforce architecture forums automatically. Upload a requirement or design, and a panel of 9 specialist AI agents debates, reviews, and delivers a binding verdict — in minutes, not weeks.

Key features

Impact analysis
Analyses each requirement and maps it to the right specialist agents before a single token is spent on review. Shows exactly why each agent is triggered.
9 specialist AI agents
Designer, LWC, OmniStudio, Flow, Apex, Patterns, Integration, Data, and Judge — each with expert-level Salesforce domain prompts across 5 editable sections.
Document upload
Upload PDF, DOCX, TXT, MD, or Confluence HTML. ARBoard auto-extracts content, detects Salesforce clouds, compliance terms, and integration patterns.
Token and cost estimator
Before each session: estimated tokens, cost range ±20%, and per-agent breakdown. After: actual vs estimated with accuracy score. Full AI spend transparency.
Judge verdict + ADR
Binding verdict: Approved, Conditions, or Revision Required. Numbered must-fix issues. Scribe agent produces a formal Architecture Decision Record with sign-off table.
Learning loop
A Learner agent extracts lessons after every session. Knowledge compounds across projects — agents improve with each review run.
